POWERLESS


Virtual, augmented, and mixed reality - the symbiosis of the digital world with the physical. There is a real intrique with the potential of the emerging technologies, as an immersion into the digital world allows for the abuility to truly immersive learning experience. However, this is also a potential danger, as these technologies may have a tendency to be an escape from percieved stress and anxiety the physical world provides.


I believe it is very important to dive into these technologies, but to develop them as a means to help people greater understand the physical, as opposed to escaping it. Here are a couple VR projects I have been working on to gain a greater understanding of this field.
