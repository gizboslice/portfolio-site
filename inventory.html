INVENTORY DATABASE



The UConn Digital Media and Design department has a program designed to inspire young aspiring computer designers and developers to become innovative. One major benifit they provide is the abuility to check out equipment like cameras, goPros, oculous rifts, phones, etc. The issue they kept having was the checkout system was extremely time consuming, difficult to use, and often very disorderly.

I was asked to join a group of developers with the task of creating an interactive database that contained a list of the equiptment, and allowed the abuility to keep track of what is available, what is out, and how long particular objects have been out for.

My job was to project manage the UI/UX team to develop a simple front end framework that was intuitive for anyone to use.
